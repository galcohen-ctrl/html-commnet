To generate your games, Como will:

• Visit your website at pancakeparlour.com.au
• Download publicly available brand assets (logo, colors, fonts, product images, menu items)
• Store these assets to skin your games and power future Como features for your brand

You can request deletion at any time via your Como account manager. By continuing, you confirm you own or have the right to use this content, and you authorize Como to use it for the purposes above.
